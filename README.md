# INNOMA.VC

## About

INNOMA.VC is an international аналитическое и консалтинговое агентство, focused on technology startups, AI-driven market analytics, and global business expansion.

The company works with startups and IT businesses across multiple industries, helping founders validate ideas, analyze markets, scale products, and enter international markets.

Founded in 2021, INNOMA.VC operates primarily in Southeast Asia, including:
- Bangkok
- Kuala Lumpur
- Jakarta
